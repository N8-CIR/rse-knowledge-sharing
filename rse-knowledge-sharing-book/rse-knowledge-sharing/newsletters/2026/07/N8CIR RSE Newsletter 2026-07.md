## Upcoming Events
### Leeds Digital Festival
Tentative date: 29th September

We are teaming up with Advanced Research Computing (ARC) at the University of Leeds is exploring the possibility of hosting a networking and knowledge exchange event as part of the  [Leeds Digital Festival](https://leedsdigitalfestival.org/) (21 September – 2 October).
The event would showcase a range of innovative digital research projects, highlight the diverse career paths available within digital research, and create opportunities for knowledge exchange and collaboration between academia and local industry.

Are you interested in presenting your work as an RSE to tech industry at the event? If so make sure to complete our [EOI form](https://leedsdigitalfestival.org/

## News

### Ai4Science Case Studies
This EPSRC funded project (October 1st 2025 to March 31st 2026) brought together dRTP expertise and N8-CIR resources to facilitate the uptake of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in scientific research.

The project carried out a number of dRTP led interventions to help researchers overcome barriers to entry and produced [six case studies](https://n8cir.org.uk/case-studies/enabling-ai-for-science/) to describe this work.
### BEDE Continues...
Following the release of the Ai4Science Case Studies, many of which make use of BEDE, we have successfully secured funding to continue BEDE access for the following institutions, Durham, Leeds, Liverpool, Manchester, Lancaster and York. This means there is still time to try out the Grace Hoppers or some of the ideas in the Ai4Science case studies above.

**Not heard of BEDE?**
Bede comprises 32 IBM Power 9 dual-CPU nodes, each with 4 NVIDIA V100 GPUs and high-performance interconnect.
Since March 2024, Bede also includes 3 NVIDIA Grace-Hopper nodes, which are available for use in an open pilot. Each node has a single superchip containing both a 64-bit ARM CPU and an NVIDIA Hopper GPU. ### N8CIR Summer Internship 2026
Following our onboarding welcome meetings with this years internship cohort they have now begun their internship journey. With a fascinating set of projects discussed we look forward to seeing their work presented at the end of the summer.
This year we have also trialled the use of the [Direct Framework](https://directframework.com/) to support our interns in identifying their current skills and competencies and to track which they develop over the summer.


### N8CIR RSE Summer Meetup 2026
This year we held the N8CIR RSE Summer Meetup in Sheffield at the Leopold Hotel. As always it was full of inspiring insights and discussions in a cosy regional scale community. We had deep dive discussions on topics such as "RSE Operations", "[ConveRSE RSE mental health](https://converse.mdsimpson.co.uk/)", "the future of Fortran", "how do we actually use AI" and "can we use the DIRECT framework to compare our skills across the N8?"
Watch this space for a mini blog post on some outputs of these discussions.
