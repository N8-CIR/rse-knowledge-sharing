## Events

### Grace Hopper Drop-in session
📆Date: 11th March
🗺Location: Online
🤔Who: Anyone who has used or is thinking of using Grace Hopper nodes (on BEDE or elsewhere).
📢We are looking for speakers or volunteers willing to share their approach and/or code.

Are you an academic researcher, HPC specialist, or RSE exploring the power of NVIDIA Grace Hopper architecture in your computational workflows? Join us for an open, interactive online event designed specifically for the academic high‑performance computing community.
This session creates a collaborative space for users of Grace Hopper systems to share successes, challenges, practical methods, and lessons learned from real research workloads.

**Full details and registration:** [Grace Hopper Drop-in session](https://n8cir.org.uk/events/grace-hopper-drop-in-session/)

### PILOT N8CIR-WHPC Tech Triaging Workshop
📆Date: 4th March
🗺Location: Online
🤔Who: post-graduates, post-docs, early career researchers, research data stewards, and research software engineers.

Join us for the PILOT N8CIR-WHPC Tech Triaging Workshop, an applied problem‑solving session where experts collaborate to unpack and roadmap an early‑stage technical challenge brought by an innovator. This interactive format—adapted from successful triage models used in the mathematical science communities—offers an intellectually stimulating way to contribute your expertise in an area that’s (probably) different from your day job.

**Full details and registration:** [PILOT N8CIR-WHPC Tech Triaging Workshop](https://n8cir.org.uk/events/pilot-n8cir-whpc-tech-triaging-workshop/)
